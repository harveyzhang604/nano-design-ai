## 四、Prompt 优化方案（本报新增，未在此前报告中覆盖）

### 4.1 Claymation — 质感细节强化（低曝光高潜力）

**背景**：Claymation 在 artsmart.ai
### 4.1 Claymation — 质感细节强化（低曝光高潜力）

**现状**：功能存在但曝光度低，趋势正在升温（artsmart.ai 报告明确点名）

**Prompt 优化建议**（在现有 prompt 基础上追加）：
```
- Handcrafted clay texture: visible fingerprints and tool marks on surfaces
- Slightly uneven, organic shape imperfections (not digitally perfect)
- Matte, non-reflective clay surface with subtle color variations
- Stop-motion-style lighting: strong directional studio light with soft shadows
- Rounded, slightly exaggerated proportions typical of claymation characters
- Visible clay seams and joints at articulation points
- Warm, tactile color palette reminiscent of Aardman / Wallace & Gromit aesthetic
```

**参数调整**：temperature 0.5→0.45，强调有机形态感

**推广行动**：在工具列表页将 Claymation 从默认排序提前，加「🔥 趋势」标签

---

### 4.2 Action Figure — 持续强化（热度最高，保持领先）

**现状**：已有优化，仍是全网最热格式，需持续迭代

**本轮优化重点**：加强「产品包装盒」细节真实感，这是近期用户最多截图分享的视觉亮点

**追加到现有 prompt**：
```
- Retail packaging box: clear plastic window panel showing figure inside
- Printed cardboard backing with character name, logo, and spec text
- UPC barcode and copyright text on packaging bottom
- Collector edition holographic sticker or serial number label
- Articulation points visible: shoulders, elbows, knees, neck joints
- Figure accessories displayed alongside in fitted plastic tray insert
- Studio product photography lighting: clean white/gradient background
```
